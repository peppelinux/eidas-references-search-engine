<div id="tit_1" class="eli-main-title">

COMMISSION IMPLEMENTING REGULATION (EU) 2025/1942

of 29 September 2025

laying down rules for the application of Regulation (EU) No 910/2014 of the European Parliament and of the Council as regards qualified validation services for qualified electronic signatures and qualified validation services for qualified electronic seals

</div>

<div id="pbl_1" class="eli-subdivision">

THE EUROPEAN COMMISSION,

<div id="cit_1" class="eli-subdivision">

Having regard to the Treaty on the Functioning of the European Union,

</div>

<div id="cit_2" class="eli-subdivision">

Having regard to Regulation (EU) No 910/2014 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 23 July 2014 on electronic identification and trust services for electronic transactions in the internal market and repealing Directive 1999/93/EC <a href="#ntr1-L_202501942EN.000101-E0001" id="ntc1-L_202501942EN.000101-E0001">(<span class="oj-super oj-note-tag">1</span>)</a>, and in particular Article 33(2) and Article 40 thereof,

</div>

Whereas:

<div id="rct_1" class="eli-subdivision">

|       |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
|-------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| \(1\) | Qualified validation services for qualified electronic signatures and for qualified electronic seals ensure the integrity, authenticity and correctness of the process and results of the validation of qualified electronic signatures and of qualified electronic seals respectively. Those qualified trust services play a crucial role in the digital business environment by promoting the transition from traditional paper-based processes to electronic equivalent ones. |

</div>

<div id="rct_2" class="eli-subdivision">

|       |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
|-------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| \(2\) | The presumption of compliance laid down in Article 33(2) and Article (40) of Regulation (EU) No 910/2014 should only apply where qualified validation services for qualified electronic signatures and for qualified electronic seals comply with the technical standards set out in this Regulation. Those standards should reflect established practices and be widely recognised within the relevant sectors. They should be adapted to include additional controls ensuring the security and trustworthiness of the qualified trust services, as well as the ability to verify the qualified status and technical validity of qualified electronic signatures and qualified electronic seals. |

</div>

<div id="rct_3" class="eli-subdivision">

|       |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
|-------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| \(3\) | If a trust service provider adheres to the requirements set out in the Annex to this Regulation, supervisory bodies should presume compliance with the relevant requirements of Regulation (EU) No 910/2014 and duly consider such presumption for granting or confirming the qualified status of the trust service. However, a qualified trust services provider may still rely on other practices to demonstrate compliance with the requirements of the Regulation (EU) No 910/2014. |

</div>

<div id="rct_4" class="eli-subdivision">

|       |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
|-------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| \(4\) | The Commission regularly assesses new technologies, practices, standards or technical specifications. In accordance with recital 75 of Regulation (EU) 2024/1183 of the European Parliament and of the Council <a href="#ntr2-L_202501942EN.000101-E0002" id="ntc2-L_202501942EN.000101-E0002">(<span class="oj-super oj-note-tag">2</span>)</a>, the Commission should review and update this Regulation, if necessary, to keep it in line with global developments, new technologies, standards or technical specifications and to follow the best practices on the internal market. |

</div>

<div id="rct_5" class="eli-subdivision">

|       |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
|-------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| \(5\) | Regulation (EU) 2016/679 of the European Parliament and of the Council <a href="#ntr3-L_202501942EN.000101-E0003" id="ntc3-L_202501942EN.000101-E0003">(<span class="oj-super oj-note-tag">3</span>)</a> and, where relevant, Directive 2002/58/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council <a href="#ntr4-L_202501942EN.000101-E0004" id="ntc4-L_202501942EN.000101-E0004">(<span class="oj-super oj-note-tag">4</span>)</a> apply to the personal data processing activities under this Regulation. |

</div>

<div id="rct_6" class="eli-subdivision">

|       |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
|-------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| \(6\) | The European Data Protection Supervisor was consulted in accordance with Article 42(1) of Regulation (EU) 2018/1725 of the European Parliament and of the Council <a href="#ntr5-L_202501942EN.000101-E0005" id="ntc5-L_202501942EN.000101-E0005">(<span class="oj-super oj-note-tag">5</span>)</a> and delivered its opinion on 6 June 2025. |

</div>

<div id="rct_7" class="eli-subdivision">

|       |                                                                                                                                                            |
|-------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| \(7\) | The measures provided for in this Regulation are in accordance with the opinion of the committee established by Article 48 of Regulation (EU) No 910/2014, |

</div>

HAS ADOPTED THIS REGULATION:

</div>

<div id="enc_1" class="eli-subdivision">

<div id="art_1" class="eli-subdivision">

Article 1

<div id="art_1.tit_1" class="eli-title">

Reference standards and specifications

</div>

The reference standards and specifications referred to in Article 33(2) and Article 40 of Regulation (EU) No 910/2014 are set out in the Annex to this Regulation.

</div>

<div id="art_2" class="eli-subdivision">

Article 2

<div id="art_2.tit_1" class="eli-title">

Entry into force

</div>

This Regulation shall enter into force on the twentieth day following that of its publication in the <span class="oj-italic">Official Journal of the European Union</span>.

</div>

</div>

<div id="fnp_1" class="eli-subdivision">

<div class="oj-final">

This Regulation shall be binding in its entirety and directly applicable in all Member States.

Done at Brussels, 29 September 2025.
