<div id="tit_1" class="eli-main-title">

COMMISSION IMPLEMENTING REGULATION (EU) 2025/1570

of 29 July 2025

laying down rules for the application of Regulation (EU) No 910/2014 of the European Parliament and of the Council as regards notification of information on certified qualified electronic signature creation devices and certified qualified electronic seal creation devices

</div>

<div id="pbl_1" class="eli-subdivision">

THE EUROPEAN COMMISSION,

<div id="cit_1" class="eli-subdivision">

Having regard to the Treaty on the Functioning of the European Union,

</div>

<div id="cit_2" class="eli-subdivision">

Having regard to Regulation (EU) No 910/2014 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 23 July 2014 on electronic identification and trust services for electronic transactions in the internal market and repealing Directive 1999/93/EC <a href="#ntr1-L_202501570EN.000101-E0001" id="ntc1-L_202501570EN.000101-E0001">(<span class="oj-super oj-note-tag">1</span>)</a>, and in particular Article 31(3) and Article 39(3) thereof,

</div>

Whereas:

<div id="rct_1" class="eli-subdivision">

|       |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
|-------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| \(1\) | To establish a transparent and reliable source of information on qualified electronic signature creation devices and on qualified electronic seal creation devices that have been certified, or that are no longer certified, by certification bodies referred to in Article 30(1) of Regulation (EU) No 910/2014, Member States are to notify the relevant information to the Commission, using a secure electronic channel made available by the Commission. |

</div>

<div id="rct_2" class="eli-subdivision">

|       |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
|-------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| \(2\) | To ensure an adequate time for the Commission to adjust the existing channel for the notifications of information as regards the certification status of qualified electronic signature creation devices and qualified electronic seal creation devices, this Regulation should apply from 3 months as of its entry into force. |

</div>

<div id="rct_3" class="eli-subdivision">

|       |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
|-------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| \(3\) | Regulation (EU) 2016/679 of the European Parliament and of the Council <a href="#ntr2-L_202501570EN.000101-E0002" id="ntc2-L_202501570EN.000101-E0002">(<span class="oj-super oj-note-tag">2</span>)</a> and, where relevant, Directive 2002/58/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council <a href="#ntr3-L_202501570EN.000101-E0003" id="ntc3-L_202501570EN.000101-E0003">(<span class="oj-super oj-note-tag">3</span>)</a> apply to the personal data processing activities under this Regulation. |

</div>

<div id="rct_4" class="eli-subdivision">

|       |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
|-------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| \(4\) | The European Data Protection Supervisor was consulted in accordance with Article 42(1) of Regulation (EU) 2018/1725 of the European Parliament and of the Council <a href="#ntr4-L_202501570EN.000101-E0004" id="ntc4-L_202501570EN.000101-E0004">(<span class="oj-super oj-note-tag">4</span>)</a>, and delivered its opinion on 6 June 2025. |

</div>

<div id="rct_5" class="eli-subdivision">

|       |                                                                                                                                                            |
|-------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| \(5\) | The measures provided for in this Regulation are in accordance with the opinion of the committee established by Article 48 of Regulation (EU) No 910/2014, |

</div>

HAS ADOPTED THIS REGULATION:

</div>

<div id="enc_1" class="eli-subdivision">

<div id="art_1" class="eli-subdivision">

Article 1

<div id="001.001">

1.   The notification referred to in Article 31(1) of Regulation (EU) No 910/2014 shall contain at least the information set out in the Annex to this Regulation and shall be made in electronic form through a secure electronic channel made available by the Commission.

</div>

<div id="001.002">

2.   Where there are any changes to the submitted information after the initial submission, including changes to the certification status of qualified electronic signature creation devices and qualified electronic seal creation devices, Member States shall submit the updated information with the relevant accompanying documents through the channel referred to in paragraph 1.

</div>

</div>

<div id="art_2" class="eli-subdivision">

Article 2

This Regulation shall enter into force on the twentieth day following that of its publication in the <span class="oj-italic">Official Journal of the European Union</span>.

This Regulation shall apply from 19 December 2025.

</div>

</div>

<div id="fnp_1" class="eli-subdivision">

<div class="oj-final">

This Regulation shall be binding in its entirety and directly applicable in all Member States.

Done at Brussels, 29 July 2025.

<div class="oj-signatory">

<span class="oj-italic">For the Commission</span>

<span class="oj-italic">The President</span>

Ursula VON DER LEYEN

</div>
